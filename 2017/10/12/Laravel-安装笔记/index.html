[Symfony\Component\Process\Exception\RuntimeException]                       
The Process class relies on proc_open, which is not available on your PHP i  
nstallation.

这是因为proc_open函数有风险，默认被禁用了。
解决方案

搜索disable_functions
删除proc_open
